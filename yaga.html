Day 1

Arrival, Welcome Circle, & First Adventure
  • 12:00pm – For Early Arrivals
    Arrive early and start your relaxation journey. Unwind by the pool, book a rejuvenating massage, or take a stroll around the beautiful villa grounds.
  • 3:00pm – Official Retreat Start
    Check-in, settle into your luxury accommodation, and soak in the tranquil surroundings.
  • 4:30pm – Welcome Circle & Intention Setting
    Meet your fellow retreat participants for a relaxed welcome circle. Set your intentions for the retreat while enjoying a healthy drink and snack.
  • 6:00pm – Introductory Yoga Session
    Ease into your first yoga practice, focusing on gentle stretching and breathing to prepare for the days ahead.
  • 7:00pm – Dinner by the Pool
    Enjoy a delicious and nourishing meal with new friends, followed by some free time to rest and recharge.

Day 2

Exploring Lanzarote’s Beauty & Mindset Mastery
  • 7:00am – Wake Up To Yoga At Dawn
    Start your day with an energizing yoga session overlooking the stunning surroundings.
  • 8:00am – Breakfast by the Pool
    Indulge in a nutritious breakfast with fresh, locally sourced ingredients.
  • 9:00am – Boat Charter Excursion
    Set sail on a luxury boat charter along Lanzarote’s breathtaking coastline.
  • 1:00pm – Lunch
    Enjoy a relaxing lunch either back at the villa or at a scenic spot nearby.
  • 2:00pm – Massage or Chill
    Take some downtime to reflect, unwind, or treat yourself to a relaxing massage.
  • 4:00pm – Micro Bootcamp Class
    Get your blood pumping with a high-energy bootcamp session, designed to challenge and invigorate you.
  • 6:00pm – Dinner by the Pool
    Savor a nutritious meal in a beautiful setting with your retreat community.
  • 8:00pm – Life Coaching Module: Goal Focus
    Learn how to set powerful, aligned goals with actionable steps during this transformative coaching session.
  • 9:00pm – Free Time/Bed
    Enjoy some quiet time to reflect on the day, journal, or simply rest.
