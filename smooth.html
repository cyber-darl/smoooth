Day 1

Arrival, Welcome Circle, & First Adventure
  • 12:00pm – For Early Arrivals
    Arrive early and start your relaxation journey. Unwind by the pool, book a rejuvenating massage, or take a stroll around the beautiful villa grounds.
  • 3:00pm – Official Retreat Start
    Check-in, settle into your luxury accommodation, and soak in the tranquil surroundings.
  • 4:30pm – Welcome Circle & Intention Setting
    Meet your fellow retreat participants for a relaxed welcome circle. Set your intentions for the retreat while enjoying a healthy drink and snack.
  • 6:00pm – Introductory Yoga Session
    Ease into your first yoga practice, focusing on gentle stretching and breathing to prepare for the days ahead.
  • 7:00pm – Dinner by the Pool
    Enjoy a delicious and nourishing meal with new friends, followed by some free time to rest and recharge.
